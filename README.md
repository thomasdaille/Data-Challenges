# DataDNA Challenges

On this challenge, we focused on automobile insurance policies.

I focused on customer profiles before turning to the type of vehicles they drive. I finished the analysis by looking for the types of customers who tended to have more costly accidents for the insurance company.

My entry was recognised and is one of the runners-up in the challenge.
